Schemes

The place for all the Government schemes providing support to the farmers, starting from providing financial aid for building their houses to developing their crop fields for a better living.

Krishi-Kshetra

Connecting Farmers and Traders: a web platform to let the farmers showcase their surplus products to various vendors and to help them connect with and sell their crops to people outside of their usual market.

MSP

MSP is the guaranteed ‘minimum floor price’ that farmer must get from the government in case the market price of the crops falls below the MSP.

Loan Calculator

Loan Calculator is a helpful thing for the farmers to calculate their loans by entering time and rate of intret. This helps them to have a clear awerness of how much money he has to pay for landlord.
